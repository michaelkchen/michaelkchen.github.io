Photography is a more recent hobby of mine that I started to pick up in my senior year of high school. I took a class that taught the basics of using a digital camera and fundamentals in design and composition. I really enjoyed making artwork directly using the world around me, but school campus was way too small of a canvas for my new ambitions.
<br/>
Since then, I have branched out more in the real world to test and improve my photography skills. I have captured many pictures in a variety of scenery, ranging from close-ups of flowers to long exposures of the night sky, and color grade them in GIMP. My journey in photography is just beginning, and I am very excited to explore the endless possibilities in this subject.
<br/>
Below, you will find a bunch of my favorite pictures ever taken, most of which using my current Canon EOS R100.
