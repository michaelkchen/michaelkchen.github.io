Photography is a more recent hobby of mine that I started to pick up in my senior year of high school. I took a class that taught the basics of using a digital camera and fundamentals in design and composition. I really enjoyed making artwork directly using the world around me, but school campus was way too small of a canvas for my new ambitions.
