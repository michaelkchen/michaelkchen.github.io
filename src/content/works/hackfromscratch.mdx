As a part of the organizer team for HackFromScratch as an editor, I produced multiple highlight reels for the event, which were posted on Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, Reddit, and X/Twitter. I focused on capturing the fun, lighthearted energy of the streams to captivate viewers into participating in the charity.
<br/>

<div style="overflow: auto">
<Image src={hfs_crew} width={width} height={height} alt="The crew of HackFromScratch." style="float: right; margin: 0.5em"/>
The brodcast itself consisted of 35 live streamed events which had a combined total of 48+ hours and over 1,000 unique viewers throughout the whole week. The whole series of events was initiated and led by my friend <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexander-kutulas/" target="_blank">Alexander Kutlas</a> with student clubs like Purdue Hackers, Purdue Music Producers, and others contributing work to the project, as well as sponsorships from Wabash Landing 9, Hyperkin, and more.
</div>

<br/>
It also consisted of a creative jam, where the audience would submit work to compete for prizes for standing out in certain categories (Visualization, Mayhem, Audio Alchemist, etc).
