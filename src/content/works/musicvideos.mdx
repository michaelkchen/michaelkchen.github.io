Being the first non-gaming videos I have worked on, these projects greatly tested my adaptability, creativity, and collaboration skills in video making. Without the freedom of recording and choosing my own media, I learned to adapt my video editing style to content that does not necessarily fit my standards of excellence. Through intention, dedication, and proper communication, however, I found that I can create work that I am proud of no matter what I am given.
