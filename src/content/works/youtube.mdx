"Electric" is a project I started back in 5th grade, aspiring to be like the content creators that I watched back then. As I continued to output more content, my knowledge along with my standards for excellence grew. Because I am an independent creator, I established key skills for the whole process of content creation. By working extensively and on a variety of genres, I exercise my passion and knowledge for crafting visually appealing and engaging media and am able to adapt to any context. 
<br/>
Currently, the channel has almost 500 subscribers and over 125,000 total views. I use OBS to record footage, Audacity for dealing with audio, DaVinci Resolve to edit, and GIMP to make thumbnails. Below are some examples of the styles I work in and some of my favorite videos ever made.
